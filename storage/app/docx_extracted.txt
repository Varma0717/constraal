Company Overview
Constraal
 is a global industrial robotics, automation and appliances company developing intelligent systems designed to operate quietly and reliably in the background of everyday life. The company builds advanced robotics and connected technologies for 
homes, smart appliances, and construction environments
, with a focus on practical automation that improves how people live and work.
Rather than creating technology that demands attention, Constraal focuses on systems that 
integrate naturally into their environments
 — delivering comfort, productivity, and safety through dependable automation.
Vision
To build a world where robotics and intelligent systems enhance daily life so seamlessly that they become an invisible but essential layer of modern living.
Mission
To design and deliver reliable, intelligent automation systems that simplify tasks, improve efficiency, and elevate safety across homes and work environments.
What Makes Constraal Different
Unlike many robotics companies that focus purely on innovation or novelty, Constraal is built around 
practical intelligence
:
Technology that solves real, everyday problems
Systems designed for long-term reliability, not short-term trends
Integration that reduces complexity instead of adding to it
Products that prioritize safety and predictability
Constraal believes the best technology is 
felt through its benefits, not noticed through its presence
.
Business Structure
🏠 Constraal Home
Constraal Home develops consumer robotics and connected home devices designed to automate routine household tasks.
Product Categories
Autonomous vacuum and floor-cleaning robots
Robotic lawn care systems
Smart home sensors and environmental monitoring devices
Ecosystem Experience
 Devices are managed through a unified mobile application where users can:
Define cleaning and operation zones
Adjust performance modes and automation behavior
Schedule routines
Customize environmental preferences
Focus:
 Seamless automation that blends into daily home life.
🍽 Constraal Appliances
Constraal Appliances develops intelligent appliance control systems that combine digital interfaces with connected automation.
Product Categories
Smart refrigerator touchscreen control systems
Connected cooking and kitchen appliances
Food monitoring and management systems
Focus:
 Minimalist, intuitive, and efficient appliance experiences that integrate with the Constraal Home ecosystem.
🏗 Constraal Build
Constraal Build is an industrial automation division dedicated to robotics for construction and infrastructure environments.
Automation Systems Include
Robotic painting systems
Drywall installation robots
Flooring and surface finishing robots
Cabinetry and precision interior robots
Pavement and demolition robots
This division operates independently from the consumer ecosystem and is designed for 
industrial-grade durability, precision, and productivity
.
Technology Platform
Constraal systems are powered by a combination of:
Robotics Engineering
 – Precision mechanical and motion systems
Computer Vision
 – Environmental awareness and object recognition
Smart Mapping
 – Spatial intelligence and navigation
Automation Software
 – Task planning, optimization, and control
Connected Systems
 – Secure device communication and ecosystem integration
Together, these technologies allow Constraal systems to operate 
safely, adaptively, and autonomously
.
Brand Pillars
Constraal products and platforms are guided by five core principles:
Reliability
Technology must work consistently in real-world environments.
Intelligence
Systems should adapt, learn, and make decisions that improve performance.
Safety
Robotics must be designed to operate responsibly around people and property.
Integration
Home and Appliance products function as a unified ecosystem, not isolated devices.
Simplicity
Advanced technology should be effortless to use.
Target Impact
Constraal aims to:
Reduce time spent on repetitive household tasks
Improve efficiency and precision in construction environments
Enhance safety through automation and monitoring
Deliver intelligent systems that make environments more responsive and easier to manage
Position in the Market
Constraal operates at the intersection of:
Consumer robotics
Smart home ecosystems
Intelligent appliances
Construction automation
By combining these domains under one technology philosophy, Constraal is building a 
multi-environment robotics platform
 designed to scale from homes to industrial worksites.
